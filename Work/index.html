1in Brackets are compatible with the REV ION System and feature #10 clearance holes on a 1/2in pitch. Using these brackets with any of our MAXTube or ION Extrusion allows for easy construction of robot frames, mechanisms, and structure.

Lap Joint Brackets provide additional versatility by enabling the effortless connection of two tubes in corner configurations or along the length of one tube, creating “lap joints.” This added functionality ensures more flexible and efficient assembly for specific structural needs.
