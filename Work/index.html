RT25 Belts are toothed belts with a 1/4in pitch are compatible with the REV ION System. These belts are comparable in strength to the belts that teams are used to using while working on the pitch of the ION build system. Belts are lighter than chain and can operate at higher speeds. RT25 provides the strength and torque capacity for mechanisms like drivetrains, intakes, and shooters.

Features

  • On pitch with the ION Build System
