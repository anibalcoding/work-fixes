MAXSpline Spacers are compatible with the REV ION System and can be used on the shaft as a spacer; they also work as spacers between wheels and sprockets, etc.

Specifications

  • OD: 1.50in
  • Weights & Dimensions:

      • Width: 0.125in
    • MAXSpline Spacer - 1/4in

      • Weight: 5.3g (0.012lbs)
