ION Grip Wheels are compatible with the REV ION System and come in a wide range of sizes, durometers, and bores. Larger wheels contain the MAXSpline bore, but can be adapted to other bores with a separate MAXHub. Wheels with spokes have a bolt circle of #10 clearance holes patterned outward at 1/2in pitch and also have a 3/8in wide nut groove, which removes the need for a wrench when utilizing the holes on the spokes. Multiple wheels can be mounted flush next to each other when more surface area is needed.
